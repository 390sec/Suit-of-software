# Student-Software-Suite

A **minimal, honest, student-focused software suite** designed to support everyday academic life without distraction, overengineering, or feature creep.

This repository contains a collection of small, independent software projects. Each project focuses on **one essential aspect of student life** and is designed primarily for **self-use**, discipline, and clarity.

---

## Purpose & Philosophy

This suite is built on a few clear principles:

* Education comes first
* Simplicity over complexity
* Awareness over control
* Support over pressure
* No distraction, no gamification, no manipulation

The goal is not to "optimize life" but to **support a basic, honest student routine**.

---

---

## Core Projects (Must-Build)

### 1. Cal-Software (Calendar & Tasks)

**Purpose:**

* Manage tasks, exams, study sessions, and deadlines

**Why it exists:**

* Every student needs a clear academic calendar
* Acts as the central planning hub

---

### 2. Leep-Software (Sleep Awareness)

**Purpose:**

* Track sleep duration and routine (information only)

**Why it exists:**

* Poor sleep affects learning and discipline
* No medical analysis, only awareness

---

### 3. Larm-Software (Alarm & Routine)

**Purpose:**

* Wake-up alarms
* Study reminders
* Sleep reminders

**Why it exists:**

* Supports routine and consistency
* Context-aware for student life

---

### 4. Oood-Software (Food & Water Habits)

**Purpose:**

* Track meals and water intake

**Why it exists:**

* Encourages regular eating habits
* No calorie tracking, no nutrition science

---

### 5. Assign-Software (Self-Assignment & Responsibility)

**Purpose:**

* Assign tasks and responsibilities to oneself
* Track progress

**Why it exists:**

* Encourages self-discipline
* Useful for personal projects and learning goals

---

## Explicitly Not Included

To maintain clarity and focus, the following are intentionally excluded:

* Social or chat features
* AI productivity scoring
* Gamification or leaderboards
* Health analytics
* Motivation systems
* Ads or notification overload
